## Why These Titles Sound Fake

These articles scored high based on sensational patterns including:

- **Apocalyptic language**: "doom", "frightening", "shocking"
- **Extreme claims**: "changed everything", "crushes", "biggest"
- **Sci-fi concepts**: "superintelligence", "AGI by 2030", "living viruses"
- **Deception themes**: "alignment faking", "deceive humans"
- **Wild predictions**: "more AI agents than people"
- **Massive numbers**: "$220M", "$750M valuation", "billions of requests"
- **Breakthrough hype**: "revolutionary", "breakthrough", "astonishing"
- **Threatening AI**: "please die", "frightening prediction"
